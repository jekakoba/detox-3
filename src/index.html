Recognizing the Crisis

Addiction doesn’t just affect your body; it takes a toll on your mind and spirit. You might be facing:

  • Intense cravings and withdrawal symptoms
  • Feelings of shame and guilt
  • Disruption in your daily life and responsibilities

These challenges can seem insurmountable, but with the right support, you can overcome them.
